Welcome!! 🌸

Hi, I'm Alyssa, a third-year Computer Science student at the University of Colorado Boulder with a focus on Software Engineering and Data Analytics. I'm also a Dean's List student with a 3.35 GPA, and I hold a Certificate of Business Administration from Front Range Community College, which gives me a practical lens for understanding how technology decisions affect real organizations.

My technical foundation spans Python, Java, SQL, JavaScript, and C++, along with tools like Microsoft Fabric, Power BI, Docker, and PostgreSQL. But more than any individual tool, I care about building things that work well and communicate clearly: whether that's a data pipeline, a full-stack application, or a dashboard that makes complex information easy to act on.

I'm currently working as a Data Analyst Consultant at Boulder Artemis Consulting, where I'm building forecasting models and Power BI dashboards for Vital Farms, helping the company understand what drives egg production at the farm level. I also serve as a Computer Science Education Lab Ambassador at CU Boulder, where I support students in navigating coursework and campus resources.

I'm excited to share that I've accepted a role as a Data Analyst at Farm Credit of Southern Colorado, starting this summer. I look forward to contributing to their work in agricultural finance and continuing to grow as a data professional.

Outside of work and school, I stay active by hiking the trails around Boulder, experiment in the kitchen, and unwind with games like World of Warcraft and Stardew Valley. This portfolio is a full picture of who I am - technically, professionally, and personally. Take a look around :D!
